What is DigiTrack?

DigiTrack is your personal productivity companion designed to keep you organized in every aspect of life. Whether it's tracking tasks, managing your journal entries, planning your month, or keeping an eye on your finances, DigiTrack simplifies everything into one easy-to-use digital platform.

With real-time functionality and seamless design, DigiTrack ensures your data is securely stored and accessible anywhere. Features like personalized to-do lists, visual monthly trackers, and daily expense monitoring make it a perfect fit for students and professionals alike.

How Does DigiTrack Work?

1. Sign In using your unique credentials.
2. Create Tasks and set monthly or daily goals.
3. Mark Completion and track your progress visually through graphs.
4. Add Financial Data daily, track income vs expenses, and see visual bar charts.
5. Secure Storage using Firebase ensures your data is always safe and synced across devices.
