About Us

DigiTrack is an innovative digital planner designed to keep you organized and on top of your goals. Whether you're managing your to-do list, keeping track of daily tasks, budgeting your finances, or setting reminders, DigiTrack helps you streamline your workflow with ease. Built on Firebase for secure and reliable data syncing, DigiTrack is the perfect tool for anyone looking to stay organized and achieve more every day.

This project was developed by Monika (4214/22) and Priyanka (4231/22), students of the final year BCA program at PGGC 11. They collaborated to create a tool that combines productivity and organization in one seamless platform.
